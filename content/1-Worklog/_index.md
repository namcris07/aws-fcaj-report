**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Basics, Identity Center, VPC Basics, Hybrid Network & Load Balancers](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [EC2 Administration, Auto Scaling, Storage (S3, EFS, FSx), Hybrid Storage Gateway & Disaster Recovery](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AWS Security Governance (IAM, KMS, Security Hub), Database Services & EKS CI/CD](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Amazon ECR Research, Image Delivery Flow & Automated Scanning](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [EKS Cluster Operations, AWS Load Balancer Controller, IRSA & Deployment Testing](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [GitOps Continuous Delivery with Argo CD, Staging Auto-Sync & Production Approval Gate](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [CloudWatch Container Insights, Observability & AWS Budget Management](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Technical Results Consolidation, Slide Deck Design & Step-by-Step Workshop Lab Drafting](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Hugo Workshop Website Launch, Content Integration, Bilingual Audit & Resource Teardown](1.9-week9/)
